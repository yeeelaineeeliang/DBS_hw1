Our Journey

Twelve Years of Moments That Mattered

Chicago, 2014 — The first Peace Club meeting
2014 · Origin
IMU Founded in Chicago

A group of high schoolers tired of invisibility decide to do something about it. They start with a circle, some chairs, and a rule: listen before you speak. The first Peace Club is born.

Origin Story
Chicago, 2015 — International Day of Peace
2015 · Celebration
First International Day of Peace

300 students across three schools gather to mark September 21st. There are performances, stories, shared meals. A tradition that now spans four cities is born in a school gymnasium.

Milestone
Midwest, 2018 — First multicultural retreat
2018 · Breakthrough
First Multicultural Retreat

100 students. 12 cultures. One weekend in nature. By Sunday evening, 94% report feeling more connected to classmates they'd never spoken to before. The proof of concept becomes proof of impact.

Impact
Chicago, 2019 — Educator workshop series
2019 · Growth
Educators Join the Movement

200 teachers complete IMU's culturally responsive practices training in year one. The same empathy work that transforms students begins reshaping classrooms from the front of the room too.

Education
Nationwide, 2026 — 38 schools and counting
2026 · Today
38 Schools. Your Move.

From one circle of chairs in Chicago to 4,200+ students across Los Angeles, Atlanta, Chicago, and New York. The mission is the same. The need is greater. The next chapter is yours to write.
