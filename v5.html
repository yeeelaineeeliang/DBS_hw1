✦ Who We Are

We're building something
real in classrooms. 💬

IMU creates brave spaces where students can actually talk about identity, culture, and race — not just read about it. No scripts, no filters. Just real students and real conversations.

We train young people to lead these circles themselves — because students listen to students. And when every story gets heard, every student thrives.

🗣️ "We don't hand students a curriculum. We ask them what they need — then we build it together."

— IMU Program Director
