Our Mission

Building Belonging,
One School at a Time

IMU is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to cultivating empathy and belonging in K–12 schools. Through Peace Clubs, International Day of Peace celebrations, multicultural retreats, and educator workshops, we create conditions where every student feels seen.

Founded in 2014 by Chicago high schoolers who believed understanding begins with listening, IMU has grown into a movement spanning 38 schools across four cities — and still growing.

"We believe in a world where every student, regardless of background, walks into school knowing they belong."

— IMU Founding Principle, 2014
Our Journey

Twelve Years of
Moments That Mattered

Chicago, 2014 — The first Peace Club circle
2014 · Origin
IMU Founded in Chicago

High schoolers tired of invisibility start a circle. One rule: listen before you speak. The first Peace Club is born from that single belief — and it changes everything.

Origin Story
Chicago, 2015 — First International Day of Peace
2015–2016 · Growth
Peace & Expansion

300 students gather for the first International Day of Peace. By 2016, Peace Clubs spread to 8 schools with student-led cross-school exchanges.

"Before IMU, I ate lunch alone every day for a year. I finally had people who wanted to know my story."

Maya R., 17 · Chicago
Midwest, 2018 — First multicultural retreat
2018 · Breakthrough
First Multicultural Retreat

100 students. 12 cultures. One weekend. By Sunday, 94% feel more connected to classmates they'd never spoken to before. Proof of concept becomes proof of impact.

"The retreat was the first time I didn't feel like I had to translate myself for anyone."

Javier M., 16 · Los Angeles
2019–2020 — Educators & virtual programs
2019–2020 · Resilience
Educators Join. Pandemic Hits. IMU Holds.

200 teachers complete culturally responsive practices training. When schools close in 2020, IMU pivots online in two weeks — 5 new cities, community intact.

Resilience
Nationwide, 2026 — 38 schools and counting
2022–Today · National
38 Schools. Your Move.
