Our Journey

Twelve Years of Belonging

2014
IMU Founded
Chicago high schoolers tired of feeling invisible launch IMU. The first Peace Club opens its doors — a place to listen before judging.
Origin Story
2014
2015
2015
First International Day of Peace
300 students across three Chicago schools gather to celebrate difference. A beloved annual tradition is born.
Milestone
2016
Peace Club Network Grows
8 schools, cross-school exchanges, and student-led dialogue sessions. The model proves it can scale.
Growth
2016
2018
2018
First Multicultural Retreat
100 students, 12 cultures, one weekend. 94% say they felt more connected to classmates they'd never spoken to before.
Impact
2019
Educator Workshop Series
200 teachers complete IMU's culturally responsive practices training in year one. The curriculum enters classrooms.
Education
2019
2020

Going Virtual
Pandemic. Schools close. IMU pivots online in two weeks and reaches students in 5 new cities. Community doesn't need a building.
Resilience

What Belonging Sounds Like

Maya R., 17
Chicago, IL · Peace Club Member

"IMU taught me that my culture is something to be proud of, not hidden. The retreat was the first time I didn't feel like I had to translate myself for anyone."
