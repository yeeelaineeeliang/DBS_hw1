Why IMU

A Twelve-Year Story of Belonging

IMU was founded in 2014 by Chicago high schoolers tired of feeling invisible in their own classrooms. The idea was simple: if students could sit in a circle and listen to each other's stories, something would change. It did.

Today, IMU serves 4,200+ students across 38 schools in Chicago, Los Angeles, Atlanta, and New York — through Peace Clubs, International Day of Peace celebrations, multicultural retreats, and educator workshops.

94% of students who participate in IMU programs report feeling more understood by their peers. Behind that number is a Maya, a Javier, an Aisha — students who finally found their table.
