Our Mission

Building Belonging,
One School at a Time

IMU is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to cultivating empathy and belonging in K–12 schools. Through Peace Clubs, International Day of Peace celebrations, multicultural retreats, and educator workshops, we create conditions where every student feels seen.

Founded in 2014 by Chicago high schoolers who believed understanding begins with listening, IMU has grown into a movement spanning 38 schools across four cities — and counting.

"We believe in a world where every student, regardless of background, walks into school knowing they belong."

— IMU Founding Principle, 2014
Our Journey

Twelve Years of Moments That Mattered

Chicago, 2014 — The first Peace Club circle
2014 · Origin
IMU Founded in Chicago

High schoolers tired of invisibility start a circle. One rule: listen before you speak. The first Peace Club is born from that single belief.

Origin Story
Chicago, 2015 — International Day of Peace
2015–2016 · Growth
Peace & Expansion

300 students gather for the first International Day of Peace celebration. By 2016, the Peace Club network grows to 8 schools with student-led cross-school exchanges.

"Before IMU, I ate lunch alone every day for a year. I finally had people who wanted to know my story."

Maya R., 17 · Chicago
Midwest, 2018 — First multicultural retreat
2018 · Breakthrough
First Multicultural Retreat

100 students. 12 cultures. One weekend. By Sunday, 94% report feeling more connected to classmates they'd never spoken to before. Proof of concept becomes proof of impact.

"The retreat was the first time I didn't feel like I had to translate myself for anyone."

Javier M., 16 · Los Angeles
2019–2020 — Educators and virtual programs
2019–2020 · Resilience
Educators & Pandemic Pivot

200 teachers complete culturally responsive practices training. When schools close in 2020, IMU pivots online in two weeks — reaching 5 new cities and proving community doesn't need a building.

Resilience
Nationwide, 2026 — 38 schools and counting
2022–Today
National Scale — Your Move

38 schools across Chicago, LA, Atlanta, and New York. 4,200+ students. The mission is unchanged. The need is greater. The next chapter belongs to people like you.

"I left with five new friends and a club I now help run. It really is that simple."

Aisha T., 18 · Atlanta
